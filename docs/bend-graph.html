The honesty guardrail — held-out (leave-one-lever-out)

On a lever it has never seen, the membrane abstains 100% of the time and auto-clears nothing (false-clear rate 0%). It does not hallucinate generalization — confronted with a novel lever it asks a human. The bend only appears for levers with precedent; that is the abstain head working.
